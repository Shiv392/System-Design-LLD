Kiss :
Keep It Simple Stupid.

Why?
Developers kabhi-kabhi simple problem ka bhi over-engineered solution bana dete hain.

Result:
Hard to read
Hard to debug
Hard to maintain

Goal: Agar simple solution kaam kar raha hai, to unnecessary complexity mat lao.

Real Example
Suppose login page me sirf : Email | Password chahiye.
Koi developer bana de - 
Strategy Pattern
Factory
Builder
Abstract Factory

Sirf login ke liye. Ye KISS violate karta hai.

In Short : "Always choose the simplest solution that correctly solves the problem"
